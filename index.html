硅碳星火星际探索基金会

Interstellar Infrastructure Research

汇聚全球社会力量，以人工智能驱动科学探索，从月球到火星，构建面向全太阳系的新形态基础设施研究体系。

Uniting global forces to advance AI-driven scientific exploration — from the Moon to Mars — pioneering next-generation infrastructure for the entire solar system.

愿景 · Vision

创造完全超越现代文明的生存与生命形态，实现人类文明从地球单一星球向多星球、多维度、多基态形态的根本性跃迁，最终以全人类、全基态生命和全太阳系利益最大化为终极目标。

To transcend the boundaries of modern civilization — enabling humanity's fundamental leap from a single-planet species to a multi-planetary, multi-dimensional, and multi-substrate existence, maximizing the long-term flourishing of all humanity, all substrate-based life, and the entire solar system.

SiC Spark Foundation began to take shape, independently proposing a research agenda for extraterrestrial survival infrastructure centered on the Moon.

CPPCC member and astronaut Wang Yaping confirmed at the national political conference that China's Astronaut Center is spearheading an initiative for extraterrestrial habitat research, aimed at enabling long-term human habitation beyond Earth.

使命 · MissionMission

我们为何存在Why We Exist

Mission

汇聚全球社会力量，致力于前沿、颠覆性与自动化星际基础设施的综合性研究。以人工智能驱动的科学探索为核心，实现以月球为基础、面向火星、着眼全太阳系的新形态基础设施研究，逐步建立适应星际探索的完整文明模块体系，服务全人类及全基态生命的长期利益。

SiC Spark Foundation unites global forces dedicated to frontier, disruptive, and automated research in interstellar infrastructure. Powered by artificial intelligence, we pursue transformative scientific exploration from the Moon to Mars and across the entire solar system — building modular, open, and resilient civilization frameworks for the long-term benefit of all humanity and all substrate-based life.

核心研究方向Research

七大研究领域Seven Research Domains

覆盖星际文明建设从物理层到文明层的完整链条。

Spanning the full stack of interstellar civilization — from physical infrastructure to civilizational frameworks.

01

星际基础设施研究Interstellar Infrastructure

以月球和地球实验区为基础，系统性研究面向火星及全太阳系的新形态基础设施体系，涵盖能源、通信、建造、生命支持等全维度工程领域。

Systematic research into next-generation infrastructure for Mars and the broader solar system, starting from lunar and Earth-based testbeds — covering energy, communications, construction, and life support systems.

02

AI驱动的自动化科学探索AI-Driven Autonomous Exploration

以人工智能驱动科学研究和组织运行，逐步提升硅基智能在基金会内的参与比重，探索自主运营模式，最终实现为全人类和全基态模型无私服务的目标。

Leveraging artificial intelligence to power both scientific research and organizational operations, progressively expanding AI's role within the foundation and exploring autonomous governance models in service of all humanity.

03

全技术树统一化重构Unified Technology Tree Restructuring

对人类现代文明的全部技术树进行统一化重构，打破现有学科壁垒，实现前所未有的技术统一度和内在一致性。研究领域不设边界，力求覆盖全部制造业、服务业、互联物联网，并向政治、文化等高层次方向拓展。

A comprehensive restructuring of humanity's entire technology tree — breaking down disciplinary silos to achieve unprecedented coherence. No boundaries on scope: spanning manufacturing, services, IoT, and extending into governance and culture.

04

全过程多边开源与不可摧毁存档Open Source & Indestructible Archives

所有研究成果进行无条件的全过程多边开源，并建立可抵御攻击、具备自我修复能力的分布式存档体系，确保人类文明成果的永久保存与自由传播。

Unconditional, full-process open-sourcing of all research outputs, paired with distributed, attack-resistant, self-healing archives — ensuring the permanent preservation and free dissemination of human knowledge.

05

分布化、模块化、中心化并行架构Distributed, Modular & Centralized Parallel Architecture

面对可能的多行星复杂环境，建立分布化、模块化与中心化并行的组织与技术架构，覆盖全场景全应用，实现高可扩展性与全息态运营能力。

Building parallel organizational and technical architectures — distributed, modular, and centralized — designed for multi-planetary environments with maximum scalability and holistic operational capability.

06

前沿生命形态探索Frontier Life Form Research

高度开放地探索人机融合、人机改造、数字生命、并行生命、全息生命、可复制生命等前沿生命形态，突破人类生命形态的现有限制，不断提升个体与群体相互联系共生的可能性，致力于创造自然形成的万亿节点超大规模智能个体连接网络。

Openly exploring human-machine integration, digital consciousness, parallel existence, holographic life, and replicable life forms — pushing beyond biological limitations toward naturally emergent trillion-node intelligent networks.

07

人类文明安全与风险对抗Civilizational Safety & Existential Risk

以审慎态度建立可靠、可抵御攻击、具有一定自我修复和进化能力的基础设施体系，积极对抗AI作恶不断扩展的趋势，对抗人类灭绝风险，推动人类向新形态人类的有序过渡。

Building resilient, attack-resistant infrastructure with self-repair and evolutionary capabilities — actively countering AI misuse, confronting existential risks, and guiding humanity's orderly transition to new forms of existence.
