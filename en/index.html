Research About Us

SiC Spark

The Fusion of Carbon-Based Life
and Silicon-Based Intelligence

For six billion years, carbon-based life evolved from single cells to civilization. In seventy years, silicon-based intelligence grew from calculators to systems that understand language. What comes next requires both.

SiC — the compound of silicon and carbon. Silicon represents computation and artificial intelligence; carbon represents biology and humanity. When these two forms of intelligence truly merge at an interstellar scale, they will create a new form of civilization beyond either one alone. SiC is our name for that fusion.

Spark — the seed of civilization. Our work begins on Earth, but points toward seeding civilization across the entire solar system. Spark is the beginning, not the end.

Why Now

Three Things Are Happening at Once

AI

AI capabilities are growing exponentially

Large language models understand and generate human language. AI can design proteins, prove mathematical theorems, write operating systems. Things unimaginable five years ago happen every month now. This means scientific research itself can be accelerated — not by 10%, but by 10x or 100x.

Space

Space is becoming a societal endeavor

Launch costs have dropped two orders of magnitude in a decade. Commercial space companies are making low Earth orbit routine. The Moon and Mars are no longer just for NASA and ESA — the whole of society can participate. But infrastructure research has fallen far behind launch capability.

Risk

Civilizational risk is at an all-time high

Nuclear weapons, biotechnology, AI misalignment — for the first time, humanity has the ability to destroy itself. Earth is a single point of failure. If civilization exists on only one planet, any sufficiently large catastrophe could end everything. Going multi-planetary isn't romance — it's necessary insurance.

The convergence of these three things has created a window: use AI to accelerate interstellar infrastructure research while creating a cross-planetary backup for civilization. SiC Spark Foundation was founded to seize this window.

What Makes Us Different

Not Another Space Dreaming Org

Open Source, Not Open-Washing

Many organizations call themselves "open," but their results are still locked behind patents and paywalls. We mean it — every research process, every dataset, every method, unconditionally public. Any person, any country can use them.

AI as Partner, Not Tool

We're not just "using AI to assist research." We're exploring how silicon-based intelligence can gradually take on decision-making and operations — de-staffing, de-founding — so the organization's survival doesn't depend on any individual.

Beyond Rockets and Spacecraft

Plenty of companies build launch vehicles. We focus on what happens after you arrive — how to live, breathe, communicate, govern, and sustain civilization on another world. This infrastructure research is currently almost nonexistent.

Thinking in Millennia

We don't chase quarterly results or annual KPIs. The foundation's organizational architecture is designed for centuries and millennia of continuous operation — distributed governance, modular architecture, self-repairing archive systems.
