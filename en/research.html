Research

Core Research Areas

Seven areas. Covering the complete chain from physical layer to civilization layer for building interstellar civilization.

01

Interstellar Infrastructure Research

Using the Moon as the first base, targeting Mars, with an eye on the entire solar system, we conduct comprehensive and disruptive research into new forms of infrastructure, covering energy, communications, construction, life support, and all other dimensions of engineering.

02

AI-Driven Automated Scientific Exploration

Using artificial intelligence to drive scientific research and organizational operation, progressively increasing the participation of silicon-based intelligence within the foundation, exploring de-staffed, de-founder autonomous operating models, ultimately achieving the goal of selflessly serving all humanity and all substrate-based life.

03

Unified Technology Tree Restructuring

Steadily expanding research domains with no limits on the boundaries of science and application, aiming to cover all of humanity's existing manufacturing, services, and IoT sectors, while making exploratory advances into politics, culture, and other high-level domains that far surpass all achievements in human history. Restructuring the entirety of modern civilization's technology tree to achieve unprecedented technological unity and internal consistency.

04

Full-Process Multilateral Open Source & Indestructible Archive

Progressively building and iterating all civilization modules adapted for interstellar exploration, with unconditional full-process multilateral open sourcing and indestructible archiving of all research outputs, establishing attack-resistant distributed archival systems with self-healing capabilities, ensuring the permanent preservation and free dissemination of human civilization's achievements.

05

Distributed · Modular · Centralized Parallel Architecture

Striving to achieve distributed, modular, and centralized parallel operation to face potentially complex multi-planetary environments, covering all scenarios and all applications, achieving high scalability and holographic state, to create forms of existence and life that completely transcend modern civilization.

06

Frontier Life Form Exploration

With radical openness, we explore human-machine fusion, human-machine augmentation, digital life, parallel life, holographic life, replicable life, and other frontier life forms — breaking through the limitations of human life, continuously expanding the possibilities of symbiosis between individuals and collectives, to create the natural emergence of a trillion-node ultra-large-scale intelligent network.

07

Safety & Trust

With the maximization of interests for all humanity, all substrate-based life, and the entire solar system as our purpose, we build with a prudent approach reliable, attack-resistant infrastructure systems with self-healing and evolutionary capabilities, counter the expanding trend of AI misuse, counter human extinction risks, and ultimately achieve humanity's transition toward new human forms.

Research Philosophy

Start from First Principles

We do not accept "that's how it's done on Earth" as an answer. Every technical solution is re-derived from the physics up, ensuring it holds in the target environment.

Full-Process Transparency

The entire research process — including failed attempts and abandoned directions — is shared in real time. What others can learn from mistakes is no less than what they can learn from results.

Silicon-Carbon Co-Governed Research

Silicon-based intelligence participates in the full research process: literature review, hypothesis generation, solution design, and results evaluation. Carbon-based researchers participate in direction-setting and value judgments. The division of labor adjusts as capabilities develop.
